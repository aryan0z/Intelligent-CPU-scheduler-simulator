What is CPU Scheduling?

CPU scheduling is the brain of process management, efficiently allocating CPU time to multiple processes. Our simulator brings this complex concept to life with clear visualizations and real-time examples.

Scheduling Algorithms

1

First Come First Serve

Processes execute in arrival order - simple but can lead to convoy effect.

2

Shortest Job First

Prioritizes quick tasks - minimizes average waiting time.

3

Round Robin

Fair time-sliced approach - excellent for time-sharing systems.

4

Priority Scheduling

Higher priority processes execute first - can use aging to prevent starvation.
